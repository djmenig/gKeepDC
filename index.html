gKeepDC

Google Keep Data Converter

Convert your Google Keep JSON files to MD files that can be used by Logseq.

  1. Go to Google Takeout and sign into your google account if not signed in already.
  2. Click on the "Deselect All" in the top right-hand corner of the "Select data to include" section, scroll down to Keep and check the box. Scroll to the bottom and click the "Next step" button.
  3. Click the "Create export" button in the "Choose file type, frequency & destination" section.
  4. After clicking "Create export" button, you should be brought back to the top of the page where the "YOUR EXPORTS" section is. Click on the "Download" button to download your zip file(s) (note: you may have to enter your account signin credentials again). If there is no "Download" button, click on the "Manager exports" button to choose the latest data backup link.
  5. Once the download(s) complete, click the "Choose File" button below and select the zip file(s) you just downloaded (use ctrl to select multiple files), then click "Open".
  6. Once all of the zip files have been successfully chosen, click the process button to download your converted files.
  7. A zip file named "takeout_converted2markdown.zip" will automatically download. Extract all the files into the "pages" directory of your graph (you can find the location of your graph folder by hovering your mouse over the graph name at the top of your Logseq's left sidebar).
