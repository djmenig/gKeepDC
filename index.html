gKeepDC

Google Keep Data Converter

Convert your Google Keep JSON files to MD files that can be used by Logseq.

  1. Go to Google Takeout and sign into your google account if not signed in already.
  2. Click on the "Deselect All" in the top right-hand corner of the "Select data to include" section, scroll down to Keep and check the box. Click the "Next step" button.
  3. Click the "Create export" button in the "Choose file type, frequency & destination" section.
  4. You should receive an email containing your download link (if not, repeat steps 2-3). Use the links contained in the email to download your zip files.
  5. Once the download(s) complete, click the "Choose File" button below and select the zip file(s) you just downloaded, then click "Open".
  6. Once all of the zip files have been successfully chosen, click the process button to download your converted files.
